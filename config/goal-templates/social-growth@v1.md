## Objective

Grow social media output on the primary channels in a way that increases reach without breaking brand or platform constraints.

## Finish Criteria

- [ ] A concrete posting batch is prepared and scheduled.
- [ ] Every scheduled post has verified copy, media, and destination details.
- [ ] Proof of scheduled or published output is saved under `proof/<goal-slug>/`.

## Context

- Primary channels and audience should be pulled from `_context.md`.
- Use the current business priorities and constraints from `_context.md`.
- Keep all work aligned with the active campaign window or launch timing.

## Constraints

- Do not mark a post complete until it is actually scheduled or published and verified.
- Respect platform rate limits, media requirements, and brand tone.
- Pause the goal if access to a required channel is missing or blocked.
